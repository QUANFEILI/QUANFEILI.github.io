Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's website is known for its sleek design, intuitive navigation, and strong branding.

  • Contrast: Impressive colors. High contrast between text and background.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts and colors throughout.
  • Alignment: Elements are neatly aligned, creating a clean look.The text and images are balanced with each other, without any sense of difference.
  • Proximity: The relevant content is well grouped and neatly formatted, making it easy for me to find where I need to go.

Overall,As an excellent benchmark for web design, Apple excels in all CRAP principles, contributing to an exceptional user experience. I love it!

Yale Art

The Yale Art website suffers from poor design and usability, making navigation difficult.

  • Contrast: A sickening arrangement of colors. Low contrast in some areas makes it hard to read text.
  • Repetition: The styles and fonts used in different parts are inconsistent, and some fonts are large or small, making it difficult to read.
  • Alignment: The elements often appear misaligned, resulting in a chaotic appearance and causing the article and background to be mixed together in a disorderly and disorganized manner.
  • Proximity: The relevant content is not effectively grouped, making user navigation complex and menu positions chaotic, making it difficult to find where I need to go.

This site fails to adhere to the CRAP principles, resulting in a frustrating user experience.
